Dear Declarant

A Route 1 means the required documents must be uploaded and attached to your declaration.

What you need to do now

If you have not already attached the required documents to this declaration, then you need to sign in and do this now so they can be checked when your goods progress.

To access the online service and upload files to the declaration with movement reference number (MRN) @params("mrn"):

  1. Search online for 'Upload documents and get messages for the Customs Declaration Service' on the GOV.UK website.

Required documents

  • certificates, permits, licences etc declared in section 5 (documents produced)
