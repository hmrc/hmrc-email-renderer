@{params("helperFirstName")} @{params("helperLastName")} has offered to be your trusted helper.

A trusted helper is someone you know like a friend or family member, who can help you with your tax online.

This offer is available to accept online for 28 days.

How to accept this offer of help

Visit the gov.uk website and search for ‘personal tax account’.

You will need to sign in with your Government Gateway password and ID to access your personal tax account.

If you do not have a Government Gateway, or a personal tax account

To set up your personal tax account, visit the gov.uk website and search for ‘personal tax account’.

You can register for a Government Gateway account when you set up your personal tax account.
