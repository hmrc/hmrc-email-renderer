How to pay

You can see how much you owe and pay by signing back into your One Stop Shop account.

    1. Search GOV.UK for ‘Submit your One Stop Shop return and pay VAT’.
    2. Sign in with the Government Gateway user ID and password you used to register for UK VAT.
    3. Select ‘View past returns’.
    4. Select your return period.
    5. Select ‘Pay now’ and choose a way to pay.
