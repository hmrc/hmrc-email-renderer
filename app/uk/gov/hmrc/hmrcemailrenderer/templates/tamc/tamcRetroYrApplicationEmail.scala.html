Confirmation of your Marriage Allowance application

What happens next

HMRC will check your eligibility before processing your application.

If your partner pays tax through PAYE, we’ll send them a cheque in the post for each tax year you are eligible for.

If your partner completes a Self Assessment tax return, we’ll send a cheque in the post or deduct the amount from their next tax bill (depending on the preference they’ve registered with HMRC).

There is no need to contact us.

You can let us know about any changes to your circumstances at https://www.gov.uk/marriage-allowance-guide/if-your-circumstances-change
