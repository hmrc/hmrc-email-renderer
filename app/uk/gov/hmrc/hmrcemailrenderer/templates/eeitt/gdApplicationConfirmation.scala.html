How to add or remove a premises

Go to GOV.UK and complete the ‘Notify HMRC about premises to include on Gaming Duty Register’ form (GD58). You also need to complete this form for premises used for gaming under Temporary Use Notices. You may be liable to civil penalties if you do not tell us about changes.
