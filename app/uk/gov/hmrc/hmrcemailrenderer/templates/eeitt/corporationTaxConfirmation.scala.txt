If you are registering a company for Corporation Tax for the first time

You will only be able to register for HMRC online services and file the company’s tax returns once you have received the company’s Unique Taxpayer Reference.

If you are re-registering a company for UK Corporation Tax

If you are re-registering for Corporation Tax we will use the information provided to update our records. You will then be able to use HMRC online services to file returns and make payments.
