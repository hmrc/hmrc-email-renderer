Alcohol Wholesaler Registration Scheme (AWRS) application submission confirmation

Your application for @{params("organisationName")} has been received on the @{params("submissionDate")}.

Your application reference number is @{params("applicationReference")}. This is not your AWRS registration number.

What happens next
- There is no need to contact HMRC, you will be notified of the outcome of your application in due course. You may not receive a decision before 1 April 2017.
- From 1 April 2017, you must not trade in wholesale alcohol if you do not have an AWRS approval from HMRC.
- If you continue to trade from 1 April 2017 without an approval you may be liable to financial penalties and your alcohol stock could be confiscated (whether or not duty has been paid).
- If approved, your business will receive an AWRS registration number confirming that you are an approved alcohol wholesaler.

More detailed information can be found in the guidance on GOV.UK.

Remember to keep your Government Gateway user ID and password safe as you will need them if you need to make changes. If your circumstances change you must update your application.

Excise Notice 2002 Section 10.4 has a full list of changes and the circumstances where you need to immediately inform us.
