What happens next
- There is no need to contact HMRC, you will be notified of the outcome of your application in due course.
- As you were trading as an alcohol wholesaler before 31 March 2016, your application should have been made before that date.
- You must not trade as an alcohol wholesaler without an AWRS approval from HMRC. You may be liable to financial penalties and your alcohol stock could be confiscated (whether or not duty has been paid).
- From 1st April 2017 it is an offence for trade buyers to buy alcohol from you.
- If approved, your business will receive an AWRS reference number confirming that you are an approved alcohol wholesaler

More detailed information can be found in the guidance on GOV.UK.
